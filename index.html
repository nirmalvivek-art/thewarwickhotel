A New Chapter in Hospitality

On February 5th, The Warwick Hotel began a new journey under the ownership of the Colombo Edition. We are proud to be the custodians of this local landmark.

We are not just maintaining the status quo; we are elevating it. You will notice gradual, meaningful enhancements throughout your stay—from the warmth of our welcome to the quality of our cuisine. We are dedicated to bringing a personal touch back to hospitality.

Thank you for choosing to stay with us as we refine and reimagine the guest experience.
